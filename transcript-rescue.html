What it does in plain English

Transcript Rescue is a desktop app that turns messy transcripts into clean, readable text in seconds. It is especially useful when your source text includes broken formatting, awkward spacing, inconsistent speaker flow, caption noise, or other transcript junk that makes editing frustrating.

Who it is for

  • Creators and podcasters cleaning rough transcript exports.
  • Researchers and consultants working from interviews or meeting notes.
  • Operators dealing with VTT captions, Zoom or Meet exports, and pasted raw transcript blobs.
  • Anyone who needs readable text fast without a full transcript platform.

How it works

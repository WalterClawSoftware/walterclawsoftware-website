Product FAQ

App Icon Creator questions.

Short answers about fit, inputs, outputs, limits, and checkout.

Who is App Icon Creator for?

App Icon Creator is for people preparing Mac or Windows desktop app icons who already have logo or artwork files and want standardized icon outputs before release.

What source artwork does it work with?

It starts from a logo PNG that you provide. The page does not claim the app creates original brand artwork.

What output formats does it create?

It creates standardized PNG app icon files, macOS ICNS files, and Windows ICO files, with small-size previews, proof sheets, and a short QA report.

Does it design a new logo or icon from scratch?

No. It standardizes, checks, and exports icon assets from artwork you provide. It does not replace a designer.

Does it guarantee App Store or Microsoft Store acceptance?

No. It helps you inspect generated icon files before release, but it does not guarantee App Store, Microsoft Store, or other platform acceptance.

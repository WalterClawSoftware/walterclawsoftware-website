This page is the plain-language store FAQ for current Walter Claw Software LLC desktop products. It is here to reduce guesswork before downloading, not to bury visitors in vague policy language.

Store link path
All five Walter Claw Software desktop apps are live on the Apple App Store for Mac and in the Microsoft Store for Windows.
Free availability
The product pages are the source of truth for free desktop availability, official store links, and any platform-specific notes.
Marketplace price refresh
All five apps have been moved to free pricing in the store dashboards. If a marketplace still briefly shows the old price, wait for the Apple or Microsoft listing to refresh or email support@walterclawsoftware.com before continuing.
Platform versions

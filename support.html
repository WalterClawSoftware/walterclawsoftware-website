What to include

How to get a faster answer.

Good support requests are specific. The more precise the report, the easier it is to fix.

Version and Windows details

Include the ClipScript version if known, plus your Windows edition and whether the problem happened after an update.

What you expected

Describe the exact action you took and what you expected ClipScript to do at that moment.

What actually happened

Include any visible error message, screenshot, export issue, sign-in issue, or transcript behavior that looked wrong.

Current product scope

ClipScript is being developed as an official creator transcript workspace.

  • Designed for Google accounts and YouTube channels the user owns or manages.
  • Focused on transcript browsing, search, local archiving, favorites, and export.
  • Intended to complement creator workflows, not replace YouTube Studio itself.

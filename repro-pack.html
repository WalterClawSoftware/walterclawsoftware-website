Repro Pack FAQ

Plain answers before you buy.

Claim-safe notes about fit, input, missing evidence, tracker handoff, and the checkout links currently shown on this page.

Who is Repro Pack for?

Repro Pack is for people who already have rough bug evidence and need a clearer handoff for support, QA, product, or development follow-up.

What kind of input does Repro Pack work with?

It works with messy support notes, customer emails, copied issue text, Slack-style reports, support threads, or other rough bug details you paste into the desktop app.

Does Repro Pack invent missing evidence?

No. The page describes Repro Pack as separating known facts from missing evidence and follow-up questions, keeping unsupported details visible instead of filling them in.

Does Repro Pack replace a bug tracker or QA investigation?

No. It prepares tracker-ready drafts for GitHub, Jira, Linear, Repro Pack output, or Markdown, but it does not replace a tracker, QA judgment, or real investigation.

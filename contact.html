Before you write

A little detail goes a long way.

The fastest way to get a useful answer is to include enough context for us to understand the situation on the first pass.

Say what you were doing

Tell us the product, page, action, export, import, or sign-in step you were using when the issue or question came up.

Include the visible result

If something looked wrong, include the error text, screenshot, transcript behavior, cleanup result, or outcome you actually saw.

Share your environment

For app support, the product name, your platform, app version, and whether you used a direct download can make troubleshooting much faster.

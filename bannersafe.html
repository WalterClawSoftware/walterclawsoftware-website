BannerSafe FAQ

Plain answers before you buy.

Claim-safe notes about fit, artwork, YouTube crop previews, export limits, and the checkout links currently shown on this page.

Who is BannerSafe for?

BannerSafe is for creators or small businesses that already have channel banner art and want a focused Mac or Windows desktop check before uploading it to YouTube themselves.

What image artwork does BannerSafe work with?

BannerSafe works with existing PNG or JPG banner artwork that you provide. It helps preview, position, and export that artwork rather than supplying a new art library.

Does BannerSafe design a YouTube banner from scratch?

No. BannerSafe is not a full design suite. It helps test and export existing banner art for YouTube crops and safe areas.

What YouTube crops or safe areas does BannerSafe help check?

BannerSafe previews YouTube banner safe zones plus desktop, mobile, and TV crop regions, then exports a 2560 x 1440 banner for you to review before uploading.

Does BannerSafe upload to YouTube or guarantee approval?

No. BannerSafe does not upload artwork to YouTube, guarantee approval, or promise that YouTube crop behavior or requirements will never change.

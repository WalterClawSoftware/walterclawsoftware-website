Privacy policy

We keep the policy simple: collect as little as possible, keep user data local where practical, and be direct about what each app touches.

This privacy policy applies to Walter Claw Software LLC, this website, ClipScript, and Transcript Rescue as they are currently designed. If a product changes in a way that materially affects data handling, this policy will be updated.

Effective date: April 13, 2026 Company: Walter Claw Software LLC Contact: legal@walterclawsoftware.com
Who we are
Walter Claw Software LLC is an Arizona software company. Our current live products are ClipScript, a desktop transcript workspace for creator-owned YouTube videos, and Transcript Rescue, a desktop app for cleaning messy transcripts into readable text on macOS and Windows.
Website data
This website is a simple informational site. We do not intentionally collect personal information through the site unless you choose to contact us by email.
Email contact
If you email us, we will receive the information you include in that message, such as your email address, message contents, any attachments you send. We use that information only to respond, provide support, or handle legitimate business communication.
ClipScript account connection
When a user signs into Google from ClipScript, the app uses Google OAuth to request access authorized by the user. That access is intended only for creator-owned YouTube content the signed-in user owns or manages.
Tokens and local files
OAuth tokens, local settings, transcript history, and exported files may be stored on the user’s own device so the app can function and reopen previous work. In the current product direction, transcript library data is intended to remain local to the device unless the user explicitly exports or moves it.
Transcript Rescue local workflow
Transcript Rescue is currently designed as a local desktop tool. Raw transcript text, opened files, cleaned outputs, digest summaries, and cleanup reports are handled on the user’s device and saved where the user chooses. The current product does not require an account login.
Direct checkout and downloads
Paid direct downloads on this website currently use Lemon Squeezy for checkout and file delivery. That provider receives the purchase details needed to process payment, send receipts, and provide download access to the purchased app files.
What we do not sell
We do not sell personal information. We are not in the business of brokering user data.
Third-party services
ClipScript may interact with Google and YouTube services when the user chooses to sign in and access creator-owned transcript data. Transcript Rescue does not require a third-party account connection in its current desktop workflow. Google and YouTube services are governed by their own terms and privacy practices.
User control
Users can disconnect their Google account in the app, remove local files from their device, and choose whether or not to export transcript material.
Children
Our products are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.
Contact
Privacy questions or legal requests can be sent to legal@walterclawsoftware.com.

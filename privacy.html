Privacy policy

We keep the policy simple: collect as little as possible, keep user data local where practical, and be direct about what each app touches.

This privacy policy applies to Walter Claw Software LLC, this website, ClipScript, Transcript Rescue, Repro Pack, BannerSafe, and App Icon Creator as they are currently designed. If a product changes in a way that materially affects data handling, this policy will be updated.

Effective date: May 6, 2026 Company: Walter Claw Software LLC Contact: legal@walterclawsoftware.com
Who we are
Walter Claw Software LLC is an Arizona software company. Our current product line includes ClipScript, a desktop YouTube transcript downloader for videos with captions; Transcript Rescue, a desktop app for cleaning messy transcripts into readable text; Repro Pack, a desktop app for turning messy bug reports into developer-ready repro packets; BannerSafe, a local YouTube banner crop-safety utility; and App Icon Creator, a local app icon creation utility.
Website data
This website is a simple informational site. We do not intentionally collect personal information through the site unless you choose to contact us by email.
Email contact
If you email us, we will receive the information you include in that message, such as your email address, message contents, and any attachments you send. We use that information only to respond, provide support, or handle legitimate business communication.
ClipScript transcript fetching
When a user fetches a transcript in ClipScript, the app uses the pasted YouTube URL or video ID to request transcript data for that specific video. The current workflow does not require Google OAuth, a separate sign-in, an API key, or a connected channel session.
Local files and transcript history
ClipScript local settings, transcript history, favorites, downloads, and exported files may be stored on the user's own device so the app can reopen previous work. Transcript library data is intended to remain local to the device unless the user explicitly exports or moves it.
Transcript Rescue local workflow
Transcript Rescue is currently designed as a local desktop tool. Raw transcript text, opened files, cleaned outputs, digest summaries, and cleanup reports are handled on the user's device and saved where the user chooses. The current product does not require an account login.
Repro Pack local workflow
Repro Pack is currently designed as a local desktop tool. Bug report text, opened files, extra notes, generated repro packets, and saved outputs are handled on the user's device and saved where the user chooses. The current product does not require an account login.
BannerSafe local workflow
BannerSafe is currently designed as a local desktop tool. Imported banner images, protected-region markings, placement adjustments, and exported 2560 x 1440 banner files are handled on the user’s device and saved where the user chooses. The current product does not require an account login.
App Icon Creator local workflow
App Icon Creator is currently designed as a local desktop tool. Source logo images, generated PNG, ICNS, ICO, proof sheets, readiness reports, and exported icon files are handled on the user's device and saved where the user chooses. The current product does not require an account login.
Direct checkout and downloads
Paid direct downloads on this website currently use Lemon Squeezy for checkout and file delivery. That provider receives the purchase details needed to process payment, send receipts, and provide download access to the purchased app files.
What we do not sell
We do not sell personal information. We are not in the business of brokering user data.
Third-party services
ClipScript may interact with YouTube services when a user requests a transcript for a video. Transcript Rescue, Repro Pack, BannerSafe, and App Icon Creator do not require a third-party account login in their current desktop workflows. Third-party services used for transcript retrieval or payment processing are governed by their own terms and privacy practices.
User control
Users can remove local files from their device, clear saved history where the app offers it, and choose whether or not to export transcript material, cleanup output, repro packet text, BannerSafe banner export files, or App Icon Creator icon output files.
Children
Our products are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.
Contact
Privacy questions or legal requests can be sent to legal@walterclawsoftware.com.

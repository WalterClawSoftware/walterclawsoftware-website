How it works

From captioned YouTube videos to usable working copy.

ClipScript is designed to reduce friction between a captioned YouTube video and the writing, editing, or planning work you need to do next.

1. Paste the YouTube URL or video ID

Start with the exact video you want to work with. ClipScript accepts common YouTube watch URLs, share URLs, Shorts URLs, and raw video IDs.

2. Fetch, read, and search

Fetch the transcript directly, review it with timestamps, search inside it, and use Open at Time when you need to jump back to a precise moment.

3. Save, favorite, and export

Keep transcripts in a local archive, mark important ones as favorites, and export the formats that fit the rest of your publishing workflow.

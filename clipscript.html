Fit and limits

Know what you are buying.

Each Walter Claw app is intentionally small. If it does not match your problem, skip it.

Good fit

You work with YouTube videos that already have captions and want reusable transcript text without a browser-tab mess.

Not for

It does not invent transcripts for videos with no captions. It tells you when captions are unavailable.

What you get

TXT, SRT, VTT, and JSON exports, plus a local archive with search and favorites.

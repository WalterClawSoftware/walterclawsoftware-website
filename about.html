About

Focused desktop tools for messy real-world work.

Walter Claw Software LLC builds workflow-first desktop software for creators and small teams. The goal is simple: turn messy inputs into clean, usable output without bloated tools, platform sprawl, or needless friction.

Desktop-first

We care about software that feels dependable on the machine where real work actually happens, not just inside a browser tab or a sprawling SaaS dashboard.

Workflow-first

We build around recurring pain points: transcript-heavy work, cleanup work, support handoffs, and the kinds of messy operational tasks people hate doing by hand.

Direct by design

We prefer direct support and straightforward product pages so buyers can understand what exists today, whether a product is available in the App Store, by direct download, or planned for a future marketplace submission.

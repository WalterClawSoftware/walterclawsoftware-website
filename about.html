About

Desktop tools for creators and small teams.

Walter Claw Software LLC builds focused desktop tools for creators and small teams doing messy real-world work. The goal is simple: turn messy inputs into clean, usable output without bloated tools, platform sprawl, or needless friction.

Desktop-first

We care about software that feels dependable on the machine where real work actually happens, not just inside a browser tab or a sprawling SaaS dashboard.

Workflow-first

We build around recurring pain points: transcript-heavy work, cleanup work, support handoffs, and the kinds of messy operational tasks people hate doing by hand.

Direct by design

We prefer direct support and straightforward product pages so buyers can understand what exists today: Apple App Store, Microsoft Store, direct download, or active store review.

Product line

The current product line is focused and real.

Walter Claw Software LLC is not presenting a giant imaginary platform.
